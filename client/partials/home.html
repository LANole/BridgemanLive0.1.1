Cool Feature of the Month - Live Stream from the International Space Station


This month's cool feature comes live from the International Space Station and NASA. If you see the "please stand by" screen do just that. These guys do switch cameras regularly and do lose signal every now and then. Also, if it's a black screen then odds are it's night and they are over an ocean. But when it's on... it's amazing. I watched the sun set from the space station the other day and while I have been blessed to see some amazing sunsets in my day... this one takes the cake.
