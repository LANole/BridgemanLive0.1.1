About Tom Bridgeman Dev

A long story short... after a decade in sales and sales management I decided to pursue a new career. I have always had an interest for IT and as I explored on-line coding courses I found I really enjoyed programming. So I pulled the trigger and walked away from one career and into another.

Starting as an account manager in the print/on-line advertising world, I worked my way into management. With the help of great leaders I was to learn the various aspects of running a business from sales to staffing, G/L's to contract negotiations.

Venturing west offered opportunities to explore the insurance and procurement industries. Fun but not my cups of tea. Ultimately, as I said above, I couldn't resist a long desire to know how to program. I've sold hundreds of thousands of dollars and have successfully managed million dollar plus operations. Now I can code. My world domination goal is beginning to take shape. Ha ha!

When I am not working towards world domination I enjoy spending time with my kids, all things tech, sports of all kinds and traveling the globe.
